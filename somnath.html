Somnath Temple

History

Somnath is revered as the first of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. The temple has been destroyed and rebuilt several times, with the present structure completed in the 1950s in the Chalukya style.

Fun Facts

  • Faces the Arabian Sea; sunset views are spectacular.
  • Sound-and-light show narrates its history every evening.
  • "Prabhas Patan" nearby hosts ancient temples and ruins.
