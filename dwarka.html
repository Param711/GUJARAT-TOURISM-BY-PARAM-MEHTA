Dwarka

History

Dwarka is an ancient coastal city associated with Lord Krishna. The Dwarkadhish Temple, with its imposing shikhara, dates to medieval times, while the site's sacred importance goes back millennia.

Fun Facts

  • One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites.
  • Bet Dwarka island is a short boat ride away.
  • Gomti Ghat steps lead down to the river meeting the sea.
