Gir National Park

History

Established in 1965, Gir is the sole natural habitat of the Asiatic lion. The reserve spans dry deciduous forests and savannahs, protected through concerted conservation since the early 20th century.

Fun Facts

  • Only place in the world with wild Asiatic lions.
  • Home to leopards, hyenas, and 300+ bird species.
  • Best time to visit: December–March; park closed during monsoon.
