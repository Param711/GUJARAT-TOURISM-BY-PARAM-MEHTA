Statue of Unity (Kevadia)

History

Dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the 182-metre Statue of Unity was inaugurated on 31 October 2018 on the Narmada River near Kevadia. The complex includes museums, viewing galleries, and landscaped parks.

Fun Facts

  • At 182 m, it is the tallest statue in the world.
  • A viewing gallery around 153 m offers panoramic dam and valley views.
  • Evening laser shows narrate Patel’s life and India’s unification.
