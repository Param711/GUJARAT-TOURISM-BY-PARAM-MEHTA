Rann of Kutch

History

The Great Rann is a seasonal salt marsh in the Thar Desert. Over centuries, trade routes and nomadic cultures flourished here. Today it is famed for the vibrant Rann Utsav festival.

Fun Facts

  • Salt desert appears luminous under the full moon.
  • Nearby villages like Hodka and Dhordo showcase crafts.
  • Little Rann hosts the Indian Wild Ass Sanctuary.
